TicTacToe

This game is for two players. One player is yellow(X), and the other is orange (O). The goal is to get three boxse in a row (across,down,or diagonally). When you enter your letter into a box and click "GO!", the box will change to your color. The game ends when a player matches 3 colors or there is a tie. Press the "Play again" button to reroll the game.
